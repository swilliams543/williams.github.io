Meet Sarah Williams

Christian Fiction and Nonfiction Author

Author Biography

Sarah Williams is a **Christian fiction and nonfiction writer**. She resides in North Carolina. She is currently teaching at the NC Pre-K level. She has been teaching for over eleven years. ***Not Rejected*** is the second motivational book that she has published. She gives all praise and glory to God for the ability to share what she has learned and seeks to bring Him glory in all that she does. And she seeks to continue to contribute literature that is both **inspiring and faith-filled**.

Latest Release: Nubia's Song

SUMMARY: The only thing that Nubia wanted to do when she went to the University was to have fun, sing in the choir as a soloist, meet new friends and focus on her studies, but the red carpet gets pulled from under her when she meets obstacle after obstacle. Is this God's plan for her? Did she make the wrong choice? All these questions plague her until she encounters God again and along the way finds the song that He always wanted her to sing.

Sarah Williams' Published Works

Egyptian Moon"

(Book 1)

SUMMARY: Set in ancient Nubia and Egypt this tells the story of an ageless tale that is sure to captivate and inspire. Hotep, a Nubian Prince, travels to Egypt in hopes of gaining military success, a luxury his father withholds from him. When his friend meets with disaster, he loses hope and is enslaved in Egypt. Mandisa, an awkward 16 year old, becomes intrigued by a stranger who presents her with a truth that could shake her foundations. When her father grows deathly ill, will she trust the stranger or rely on the old ways? When their two worlds collide, can Mandisa help Hotep to lay aside his pain and chase his dreams? Or will he hold onto his pain? Will Mandisa accept the truth and trust in someone she cannot see?

(Book 2)

SUMMARY In the second installment, Mandisa’s character is brought to the forefront. With growing feelings mounting towards Iuty who seems to see her as a kid sister, all seems to be well until her aunt’s son is abducted. When many peasants start being abducted and the government officials neglect to send out an adequate search party, Mandisa endeavours to take things into her own hands. Will her outspoken nature cause trouble or will her petition to Pharoah himself cause justice to prevail?

(Book 3)

SUMMARY In the last and final installment, Mandisa is excited to explore her newfound faith, but in the midst of her growing relationship with God, she makes a poor decision on behalf of Iuty which causes a rift between them. Will this abrupt separation cause devastation or will it be the catapulting factor that leads Iuty to his destiny and causes the lost to be found?

Nubia's Song

Not Rejected

SUMMARY Have you been an constant recipient of rejection throughout your life? Have you wondered why this has happened to you? Do you find yourself trapped with a poor self image as a result and unable to rise above the pain of rejection and create new healthy relationships with others? Then I implore you to read this how-to book which will help you identify the negative effects that rejection has had in your life and the way to overcome it through the power of Jesus Christ.

7 Myths to Avoid...

SUMMARY Do you feel like you've been waiting for Mr. Right for too long? Have you been tempted to date, flirt, or gain attention from Mr. Wrong? If you have, obtaining substitutes while waiting is just one of the several myths that are explored in this book. If you're single and waiting, get ready to learn how to become equipped with God's word, which combats Satan's lies, so that you can actively replace them with God's word.
