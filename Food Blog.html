Asian Kale Kicker Rice Mix

Prep/Cook: 20 Mins
Superfood: Kale, Whole Wheat Rice

Items Needed:

  • 5 Organic Dinosaur Kale Strips
  • Organic Whole Wheat Rice (cooked)
  • Bag of Organic Mixed Vegetables
  • 1 Organic Chicken Breast (cooked, chopped) or Grass-Fed Shaved Beef
  • 1 teaspoon of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 onion chopped, and 3 peeled garlic cloves chopped up
  • Coconut Aminos (for flavor)
  • Pepper (to taste)

Directions:

  1. Chop up your kale in a bowl and set it aside. Cook your rice (if not already cooked) according to the label's directions and set it aside.
  2. In a wok or frying pan, add the Extra Virgin Olive Oil and let it heat up over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onions and garlic and let it brown for a few minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Next, add your cooked rice, and then add your frozen mixed vegetables.
  5. Lastly, add the chopped chicken (or beef) and the cut-up kale. Cook for just a few minutes to keep the nutrients locked in the kale.
  6. Finish by adding some pepper and coconut aminos for a savory, Asian-inspired dish. Enjoy!

Splashing Fruity Yellow Smoothie

Splashing Fruity Yellow Smoothie

Superfood: Strawberries

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of frozen mango
  • 1 cup of frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup of pineapples
  • 2 cups of almond milk or organic, grass-fed milk
  • A splash of maple syrup

Directions:

  1. Add the mango, strawberries, pineapples, almond milk, and maple syrup to a high-speed blender and blend it for a few minutes until it's thick or thin, according to your taste.
  2. Pour it into a glass and finally take a sip and enjoy this fruity delight!
  3. *Please use organic or Non-GMO ingredients.

Note: This smoothie is very delicious!

ü´ê Simple Blueberry Pie

Superfood: Blueberries

Ingredients

  • 1 **Pre-made Gluten-Free Pie Crust** (9-inch)
  • 1 jar of **Sugar-Free Blueberry Jam**
  • 1 bag **Frozen Blueberries**

Chef's Note:

For the best results, please source **Organic or Non-GMO** ingredients for the pie crust, jam, and frozen blueberries.

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to **350¬∞F (175¬∞C)**.
  2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine the entire jar of sugar-free blueberry jam and the bag of frozen blueberries. Mix gently until the blueberries are coated.
  3. Pour the blueberry and jam mixture directly into the pre-made Gluten-Free Pie Crust. Spread the filling evenly.
  4. Place the pie in the preheated oven and allow it to bake for **one hour**.
  5. Remove the pie when the filling is bubbly and the crust is golden brown. Allow to cool completely before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
